York U Go - Orientation Ad

This Ad was designed to advertise York University's Orientation Week events. Was promoted via official University social media, achieving over 25,000 views on Facebook alone.

My role: Assistant filming, directing, storyboarding and editing. Lead video effects and motion graphics.
Challenges I overcame include stabilizing video to phones with non-ideal keying setup, stabilizing graphics to overall landscapes, developing expressions to fit the video's needs, and working with the other Directors to adjust shots for ideal motion graphics opportunities.

  • Client/Organization: SCLD
  • Date: August 2016
  • Service: Motion graphics & Special Effects

Ontario Provincial Vote Ad

We hope you're as excited to vote as our co-directors! 😎 #ontarioelection #ontariovotes @sandratrinity @alantrinity

A post shared by Trinity Theatre (@trinitytheatre) on Jun 6, 2018 at 1:12pm PDT

This advertisement encourages our younger audience to vote in the Provincial Elections. Character art was partially designed by a co-op student that I was in charge of. Then I combined it into a full body rig, animated it into a social media graphic, and posted it. The two characters are designed after the company's Co-Directors.

My role: Art Direction, Animation, Art Asset Organizing

  • Client/Organization: Trinity Theatre
  • Date: June 2018
  • Service: Motion graphics & Animation
